Adhyāya 13

1 isake bad tamar nam aek sundaree jo daud ke putra abashaalom kee baahin thee, us par daud ka putra amnon mohit hua.
2 aur amnon apanee baahin tamar ke karan aeesa vikal ho gaya ki beemar pad gaya kyonaki vah kumaree thee, aur usake sath kuchh karana amnon ko kattnai jan padta tha.
3 amnon ke yonadab nam aek mitra tha, jo daud ke bhai shaima ka betta tha aur vah bada chatur tha.
4 aur us ne amnon se kaha, he rajakumar, kya karan haai ki too prati din aeesa dubala hota jata haai kya too mujhe n bataaega? amnon ne us se kaha, maain to apane bhai abashaalom kee baahin tamar par mohit hoon.
5 yonadab ne us se kaha, apane palang par lettkar beemar ban ja aur jab tera pita tujhe dekhne ko aa, tab us se kahana, meree baahin tamar akar mujhe rottee khailaa, aur bhojan ko mere samhane banaa, ki maain usako dekhkar usake hath se khaun.
6 aur amnon lettkar beemar bana aur jab raja use dekhne aya, tab amnon ne raja se kaha, meree baahin tamar akar mere dekhte do pooree banaa, ki maain usake hath se khaun.
7 aur daud ne apane ghar tamar ke pas yah kahala bheja, ki apane bhai amnon ke ghar jakar usake liye bhojan bana.
8 tab tamar apane bhai amnon ke ghar gai, aur vah pada hua tha. tab us ne atta lekar goondha, aur usake dekhte pooriyan. pakai.
9 tab us ne thal lekar unako usake liye parosa, parantu us ne khane se inakar kiya. tab amnon ne kaha, mere as pas se sab logon ko nikal do, tab sab log usake pas se nikal gaae.
10 tab amnon ne tamar se kaha, bhojat ko kottree men le a, ki maain tere hath se khaun. to tamar apanee banai hui pooriyon ko uttakar apane bhai amnon ke pas kottree men le gai.
11 jab vah unako usake khane ke liye nikatt le gai, tab us ne use pakadkar kaha, he meree baahin, a, mujh se mila.
12 us ne kaha, he mere bhai, aeesa nahee, mujhe bhrashtt n kara kyonki israael men aeesa kam hona naheen chahiye aeesee mooddhta ka kam n kara.
13 aur fir maain apanee namadharai liye huae kahan jaungee? aur too israaaeliyon men aek mooddh gina jaaega. too raja se batacheet kar, vah mujh ko tujhe byah dene ke liye mana n karega.
14 parantu us ne usakee n sunee aur us se balavan hone ke karan usake sath kukarma karake use bhrashtt kiya.
15 tab amnon us se atyant baair rakhne laga yahan tak ki yah baair usake paahile moh se baddhkar hua. tab amnon ne us se kaha, uttkar chalee ja.
16 us ne kaha, aeesa nahee, kyonki yah baddha upadrav, arthata mujhe nikal dena us pahile se baddhkar haai jo too ne mujh se kiya haai. parantu us ne usakee n sunee.
17 tab us ne apane tthaluae javan ko bulakar kaha, is sree ko mere pas se bahar nikal de, aur usake peechhe kivad men chittkanee laga de.
18 vah to rangaabirangee kuta pahine thee kyonaki jo rajakumaariyan kunvaree rahatee theen ve aeese hee vasr pahinatee theen. so amnon ke tthaluae ne use bahar nikalakar usake peechhe kivad men chittkanee laga dee.
19 tab tamar ne apane sir par rakh dalee, aur apanee rangaabirangee kuta ko faaddh dala aur sir par hath rakhe chillatee hui chalee gai.
20 usake bhai abashaalom ne us se poochha, kya tera bhai amnon tere sath raha haai? parantu ab, he meree baahin, chup rah, vah to tera bhai haai is bat kee chinta n kara. tab tamar apane bhai abashaalom ke ghar men man mare baaittee rahee.
21 jab ye sab baten daud raja ke kan men padee, tab vah bahut jhunjhla utta.
22 aur abashaalom ne amnon se bhla-bura kuchh n kaha, kyonaki amnon ne usakee bahin tamar ko bhrashtt kiya tha, is karan abashaalom us se gharana rakhta tha.
23 do parsha ke bad abashaalom ne aepraaim ke nikatt ke balhasor men apanee bhedonka un kataravaya aur abashaalom ne sab rajakumaron ko nevata diya.
24 vah raja ke pas jakar kahanalaga, binatee yah haai, ki tere das kee bhedon ka un katara jata haai, isaliye raja apane karmachariyo samet apane das ke sang chale.
25 raja ne abashaalom se kaha, he mere bette, aeesa naheen ham sab n chalenge, aeesa n ho ki tujhe aadhaik kashtt ho. tab abashaalom ne use binatee karake dabaya, parantu us ne jane se inakar kiya, taubhee use ashaeervad diya.
26 tab abashaalom ne kaha, yadi too naheen to mere bhai amnon ko hamare sang jane de. raja ne us se poochha, vah tere sang kyon chale?
27 parantu abashaalom ne use aeesa dabaya ki us ne amnon aur sab rajakumaron ko usake sath jane diya.
28 au abashaalom ne apane sevako ko agyaa dee, ki savadhaan raho aur jab amnon dakhmadha peekar nasho men a jaa, aur maain tum se kahoo, amnon ko mar dalana. kya is agyaa ka denevala maain naheen hoon? hiyav bandhakar purushaarth karana.
29 to abashaalom ke sevakon ne amnon ke sath abashaalom kee agyaa ke anusar kiya. tab sab rajakumar utt khde hua, aur apane apane khchchar par chaddhkar bhag gaae.
30 ve marga hee men the, ki daud ko yah samachar mila ki abashaalom ne sab rajakumaron ko mar dala, aur un men se aek bhee naheen bacha.
31 tab daud ne uttkar apane vasr faade, aur boomi par gir pada, aur usake sab karmacharee vasr faade huae usake pas khde rahe.
32 tab daud ke bhai shaima ke putra yanadab ne kaha, mera prabhu yah n samajhe ki sab javan, arthata rajakumar mar dale gaae haai, keval amnon mara gaya haai kyonki jis din us ne abashaalom kee bahin tamar ko bhrashtt kiya, usee din se abashaalom kee agyaa se aeesee hee bat ttnee thee.
33 isaliye ab mera prabhu raja apane man men yah samajhkar ki sab rajakumar mar gaae udas n ho kyonaki keval amnon hee mar gaya haai.
34 itane men abashaalom bhag gaya. aur jo javan pahara deta th us ne ankhen uttakar dekha, ki peechhe kee or se pahad ke pas ke marga se bahut log chale a rahe haain.
35 tab yonadab ne raja se kaha, dek, rajakumar to a gaae haain jaaisa tere das ne kaha tha vaaisa hee hua.
36 vah kah hee chuka tha, ki rajakumar pahunch gaa, aur chilla chillakar rone lage aur raja bhee apane sab karmachariyon samet bilakh bilakh kar rone laga.
37 abashaalom to bhagakar gashoor ke raja ammeehoor ke putra talmaai ke pas gaya. aur daud apane putra ke liye din din vilap karata raha.
38 jab abashaalom bhagakar gashoor ko gaya, tab pahan teen parsha tak raha.
39 aur daud ke man men abashaalom ke pas jane kee badee lalasa rahee kyonki amnon jo mar gaya tha, is karan us ne usake vishay men shaantai pai.
