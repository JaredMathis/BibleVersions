Adhyāya 21

1 raja ka man naliyon ke jal kee nai yahova ke hath men rahata haai, jidhar vah chahata udhar usako faer deta haai.
2 manushy ka sara chalachalan apanee draashtti men to tteek hota haai, parantu yahova man ko janchata haai,
3 dharma aur nyay karana, yahova ko baalidan se aadhaik achchha lagata haai.
4 chaddhee ankhe, ghamandee man, aur dushtton kee khetee, teenon papamay haain.
5 kamakajee kee kalpanaon se keval labh hota haai, parantu utavalee karanevale ko keval ghatttee hotee haai.
6 jo dhan joott ke dara praapt ho, vah vayu se ud janevala kuhara haai, usake ddoonddhnevale mratyu hee ko ddoonddhte haain.
7 jo upadrav dushtt log karate haai, us se unheen ka nash hota haai, kyonaki ve nyay ka kam karane se inakar karate haain.
8 pap se lade huae manushy ka marga bahut hee tteddha hota haai, parantu jo pavitra haai, usaka karma seedha hota haai.
9 lambe-chaude ghar men jhgadaloo patnee ke sang rahane se chht ke kone par rahana uttm haai.
10 dushtt jan burai kee lalasa jee se karata haai, vah apane padosee par anugrah kee drashtti nahee karata.
11 jab ttttha karanevale ko dand diya jata haai, tab bhola buddhmaian ho jata haai aur jab buaddhmaian ko upadesh diya jata haai, tab vah gyaan praapt karata haai.
12 udharmee jan dushtton ke gharane par buaddhmaianee se vichar karata haai ishvar dushtton ko buraiyon men ulatt deta haai.
13 jo kangal kee dohai par kan n de, vah ap pukarega aur usakee sunee n jaaegee.
14 gupt men dee hui bhentt se krodha ttnda hota haai, aur chupake se dee hui ghoos se badee jalajalahatt bhee thamatee haai.
15 nyay ka kam, karana dharmee ko to anand, parantu anarthkaariyon ko vinash hee ka karan jan padta haai.
16 jo manushy buaddh ike marga se bhttk jaa, usaka ttkaiana mare huon ke beech men hoga.
17 jo ragarang se praeeati rakhta haai, vah kangal hota haai aur do dakhmadha peene aur tel lagane se praeeti rakhta haai, vah dhanee naheen hota.
18 dushtt jan dharmee kee chhudautee ttharata haai, aur vishvasaghaatee seedho logon kee santee dand bhogate haain.
19 jhgadaloo aur chiddhnevalee patnee ke sang rahane se jangal men rahana uttm haai.
20 buaddhmaian ke ghar men uttm dhan aur tel paae jate haai, parantu moorkh unako uda dalata haai.
21 jo dharma aur krapa ka peechha pakadta haai, vah jeevan, dharma aur maahima bhee pata haai.
22 buaddhmaian shooraveeron ke nagar par chaddhkar, unake bal ko jis par ve bhrosa karate haai, nash karata haai.
23 jo apane munh ko vash men rakhta haai vah apane praan ko vipaatiayon se bachata haai.
24 jo aabhaiman se rosh men akar kam karata haai, usaka nam abhaimanee, aur anhakaree ttttha karanevala padta haai.
25 alasee apanee lalasa hee men mar jata haai, kyonaki usake hath kam karane se inkar karate haain.
26 koi aeesa haai, jo din bhr lalasa hee kiya karata haai, parantu dharmee lagatar dan karata rahata haai.
27 dushtton ka baalidan gharanait lagata haai vishosh karake jab vah mahapap ke nimitt chaddhata haai.
28 jootta sakshaee nash hota haai, jis ne jo suna haai, vahee kahata hua sthiar rahega.
29 dushtt manushy kattor mukh ka hota haai, aur jo seedha haai, vah apanee chal seedhaee karata haai.
30 yahova ke virooddh n to kuchh buddh,i aur n kuchh samaj, n koi yuaktai chalatee haai.
31 yuddh ke din ke liye ghaeda taaiyar to hota haai, parantu jay yahova hee se milatee haai..
