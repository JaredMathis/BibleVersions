Adhyāya 34

1 fir aeleehoo yon kahata gaya
2 he buaddhimano ! meree baten suno, aur he gyaaniyo ! meree baton par kan lagao
3 kyonaki jaaise jeebh se chakha jata haai, vaaise hee vachan kan se parakhe jate haain.
4 jo kuchh tteek haai, ham apane liye chun len jo bhla haai, ham apas men samajh boojh len.
5 kyonaki ayyoob ne kaha haai, ki maain nidash hoo, aur ishvar ne mera haka mar diya haai.
6 yadhypi maain sachchai par hoo, taubhee jootta ttharata hoo, maain niraparadha hoo, parantu mera ghaav asadhy haai.
7 ayyoob ke tuly kaun shooraveer haai, jo ishvar kee ninda panee kee nai peeta haai,
8 jo anarth karanevalon ka sath deta, aur dushtt manushyon kee sangati rakhta haai?
9 us ne to kaha haai, ki manushy ko is se kuchh labh naheen ki vah anand se parameshvar kee sangati rakhe.
10 isaliaee he samajhvalo ! meree suno, yah sambhv naheen ki ishvar dushttta ka kam kare, aur sarvashakitaman burai kare.
11 vah manushy kee karanee ka fal deta haai, aur pratyek ko apanee apanee chal ka fal bhugatata haai.
12 nisandeh ishvar dushttta naheen karata aur n sarvashaktaiman anyay karata haai.
13 kis ne prathvee ko usake hath men saunp diya? va kis ne sare jagat ka prabandha kiya?
14 yaadi vah manushy se apana man hattaye aur apana atma aur shvas apane hee men samett le,
15 to sab dehadhaaree aek sang nash ho jaaenge, aur manushy fir mittee men mil jaaega.
16 isaliye isako sunakar samajh rak, aur meree in baton par kan laga.
17 jo nyay ka baairee ho, kya vah shaasan kare? jo poorn dhama haai, kya too use dushtt ttharaaega?
18 vah raja se kahata haai ki too neech haai aur pradhaanon se, ki tum dushtt ho.
19 ishvar to haakimon ka paksha naheen karata aur dhanee aur kangal donon ko apane banaae huae janakar un men kuchh bhed naheen karata.
20 adhaee rat ko pal bhr men ve mar jate haai, aur praja ke log hilaae jate aur jate rahate haain. aur pratapee log bina hath lagaae utta liae jate haain.
21 kyonaki ishvar kee ankhen manushy kee chalachalan par lagee rahatee haai, aur vah usakee saree chal ko dekhta rahata haai.
22 aeesa andhiayara va ghaer andhakar kaheen naheen haai jis men anarth karanevale chhpai saken.
23 kyonaki us ne manushy ka kuchh samay naheen ttharaya taaki vah ishvar ke sammukh adalat men jaae.
24 vah bade bade balavanon ko bina muchhpachh ke choor choor karata haai, aur unake sthan par auron ko khda kar deta haai.
25 isaliye ki vah unake kamon ko bhlee bhanati janata haai, vah unhen rat men aeesa ulatt deta haai ki ve choor choor ho jate haain.
26 vah unhen dushtt janakar sabhon ke dekhte marata haai,
27 kyonaki unhon ne usake peechhe chalana chhod diya haai, aur usake kisee marga par chitt n lagaya,
28 yahan tak ki unake karan kangalon kee dohai us tak pahunchee aur us ne deen logon kee dohai sunee.
29 jab vah chaain deta to use kaun doshaee tthara sakata haai? aur jab vah munh faer le, tab kaun usaka darshan pa sakata haai? jaati bhr ke sath aur akele manushy, donon ke sath usaka barabar vyavahar haai
30 taaki bhktaiheen rajy karata n rahe, aur praja fande men fansai n jaae.
31 kya kisee ne kabhee ishvar se kaha, ki maain ne dand saha, ab maain bhvishy men burai n karoonga,
32 jo kuchh mujhe naheen soojh padta, vah too mujhe sikha de aur yaadi maain ne tteddha kam kiya ho, to bhavishy men vaaisa n karoonga?
33 kya vah tere hee man ke anusar badala paae kyonki too us se aprasann haai? kyonaki tujhe nirny karana haai, n ki mujhe is karan jo kuchh tujhe samajh padta haai, vah kah de.
34 sab gyaanee purush varan jitale buddhiman meree sunate haain ve mujh se kahenge, ki
35 ayyoob gyaan kee baten naheen kahata, aur n usake vachan samajh ke sath hote haain.
36 bhla hota, ki ayyoob ant tab pareeksha men rahata, kyonki us ne anaathairyon ke se uttar diae haain.
37 aur vah apane pap men virodha baddhata haai or hamare beech talee bajata haai, aur ishvar ke piruddh bahut see baten banata haai.
