Adhyāya 35

1 yahova ka yah vachan mere pas pahuncha,
2 he manushy ke santan, apana munh seir pahad kee or karake usake viruddh bhavishyadanee kar,
3 aur us se kah, parameshvar yahova yon kahata haai, he seir pahad, maain tere viruddh hoo aur apana hath tere viruddh baddhakar tujhe ujad hee ujad kar doonga.
4 maain tere nagaron ko khndhar kar doonga, aur too ujad ho jaaega tab too jan lega ki maain yahova hoo.
5 kyonaki too israaaeliyon se yuga-yug kee shatrauta rakhta tha, aur unakee vipaattai ke samay jab unake adharma ke dand ka samay pahuncha, tab unhen talavar se mare jane ko de diya.
6 isaliye parameshvar yahova kee yah vanee haai, mere jeevan kee saugandha, maain tujhe hatya kiae jane ke liye taaiyar karoonga or koon tera peechha karega too to koon se n ghainata tha, as karan koon tera peechha karega.
7 is reeati maain seir pahad ko ujad hee ujad kar doonga, aur jo us men ata-jata ho, maain usako nash karoonga.
8 aur maain usake pahadon ko mare huon se bhr doonga tere tteelo, taraiyon aur sab nalon men talavar se mare huae girenge !
9 maain tujhe yug yug ke liye ujad kar doonga, aur tere nagar fir n basenge. tab tum jan lage ki maain yahova hoo.
10 kyonaki too ne kaha haai, ki ye donon jaatiyan aur ye donon desh mere honge aur ham hee unake svamee ho jaaenge, yadhyapi yahova vahan tha.
11 is kara, parameshvar yahova kee yah vanee haai, mere jeevan kee sauganadha, tere kop ke anusar, aur jo jalajalahatt too ne un par apane vaair ke karan kee haai, usee ke anusar maain tujh se bartav karoonga, aur jab maain tera nyay karoo, tab tum men apane ko pragatt karoonga.
12 aur too janega, ki mujh yahova ne teree sab tiraskar kee baten sunee haai, jo too ne israaael ke pahadon ke vishay men kahee, ki, ve to ujad gaa, ve ham hee ko diae gaae haain ki ham unhen kha dalen.
13 tum ne apane muunh se mere viruddh badai maree, aur mere viruddh bahut baten kahee haain ise maain ne suna haai.
14 parameshvar yahova yon kahata haai, jab prathvee bhr men anand hoga, tab maain tujhe ujad karoonga.
15 too israaael ke gharane ke nij bhag ke ujad jane ke karan anaandait hua, so maain bhee tujh se vaaisa hee karoonga he seir pahad, he aedom ke sare desha, too ujad ho jaaega. tab ve jan lenge ki maain yahova hoo.
