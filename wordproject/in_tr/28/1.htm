Adhyāya 1

1 yahooda ke raja ujjaiyah, yotam, ahaj, aur hijaakiyyah ke dinon men aur israael ke raja yoash ke putra yarobam ke dinon me, yahova ka vachan beree ke putra hosho ke pas pahuncha..
2 jab yahova ne hosho ke dara paahile paahil baten kee, tab us ne hosho se yah kaha, jakar aek veshya ko apanee patnee bana le, aur usake kukarma ke ladkebalon ko apane ladkebale kar le, kyonaki yah desh yahova ke peechhe chalana chhodkar veshya ka sa bahut kam karata haai.
3 so us ne jakar dibalaaim kee bettee gomer ko apanee patnee kar liya, aur vah us se garbhvatee hui aur usake putra utpann hua.
4 tab yahova ne us se kaha, usaka nam yijraail rakh kyonaki thode hee kal men maain yehoo ke gharane ko yijraail kee hatya ka dand doonga, aur maain israael ke gharane ke rajy ka ant kar doonga.
5 aur us samay maain yijraail kee tarai men israael ke dhanush ko tod daloonga..
6 aur vah sree fir garbhvatee hui aur usake aek bettee utpann hui. tab yahova ne hosho se kaha, usaka nam loroohama rakh kyonki maain israael ke gharane men fir kabhee daya karake unaka aparadha kisee prakar se kshama n karoonga.
7 parantu yahooda ke gharane par maain daya karoonga, aur unaka uddhar karoonga unaka uddhar maain dhanush va talavar va yuddh va ghaedon va savaron ke dara nahee, parantu unake parameshvar yahova ke dara karoonga..
8 jab us sree ne loroohama ka dudha chhudaya, tab vah garbhvatee hui aur us se aek putra utpann hua.
9 tab yahova ne kaha, isaka nam loammee rakh kyonki tum log meree praja naheen ho, aur n maain tumhara parameshvar rahoonga..
10 taubhee israaeliyon kee ginatee samudra kee baloo kee see ho jaaegee, jinaka mapana-aginana anahona haai aur jis sthan men un se yah kaha jata tha ki tum meree praja naheen ho, usee sthan men ve jeeavit parameshvar ke putra kahalaaenge.
11 tab yahoodee aur israaelee donon ikatthe ho apana aek pradhaan ttharakar desh se chale aaenge kyonki yijrael ka din prasiddh hoga.
