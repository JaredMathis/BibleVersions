Adhyāya 119

1 kya hee dhany haain ve jo chal ke khre haai, aur yahova kee vyavastha par chalate haain!
2 kya hee dhany haain ve jo usakee chitauaniyon ko manate haai, aur poorn man se usake pas ate haain!

10 maain poore man se teree khoj meen laga hoon mujhe teree agyaaon kee batt se bhttkane n de!

30 maain ne sachchai ka marga chun liya haai, tere niyamon kee or maain chitt lagaae rahata hoon.
31 maain teree chitauaniyon men lavaleen hoo, he yahova, meree asha n tod!
