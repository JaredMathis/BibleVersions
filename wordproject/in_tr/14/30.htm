Adhyāya 30

1 fir hijaakiyyah ne sare israaael aur yahooda men kahala bheja, aur aepraaim aur manashsho ke pas is ashay ke patra likh bheje, ki tum yarooshalem ko yahova ke bhvan men israaael ke parameshvar yahova ke liye fasah manane ko ao.
2 raja aur usake hakimon aur yarooshalem kee mandlee ne sammaati kee thee ki fasah ko doosare maheene men manaaen.
3 ve use us samay is karan n mana sakate the, kyonki thode hee yajakon ne apane apane ko pavitra kiya tha, aur praja ke log yarooshalem men ikatthe n huae the.
4 aur yah bat raja aur saree mandlee ko achchhee lagee.
5 tab unhon ne yah tthara diya, ki bershoba se lekar dan ke sare israaaeliyon men yah prachar kiya jay, ki yarooshalem men israaael ke parameshvar yahova ke liye fasah manane ko chale ao kyonki unhon ne itanee badee sankhya men usako is prakar n manaya tha jaaisa ki likha haai.
6 isaliye harakare raja aur usake haakimon se chitthiyan lekar, raja kee agyaa ke anusar sare israaael aur yahooda men ghoome, aur yah kahate gaa, ki he israaaeliyo ! ibraaheem, isahak aur israaael ke parameshvar yahova kee or firo, ki vah ashshoor ke rajaon ke hath se bache huae tum logaen kee or fire.
7 aur apane purakhaon aur bhaiyon ke saman mat bano, jinhon ne apane poorvajon ke parameshvar yahova se vishvasaghaat kiya tha, aur us ne unhen chaakit hone ka karan kar diya, jaaisa ki tum svayan dekh rahe ho.
8 ab apane purakhaon kee nai hatt n karo, varan yahova ke adhaeen hokar usake us paavitrasthan men ao jise us ne sada ke liye paavitra kiya haai, aur apane parameshvar yahova kee upasana karo, ki usaka bhdka hua krodha tum par se door ho jaae.
9 yaadi tum yahova kee or firoge to jo tumhare bhaiyon aur ladkebalon ko bandhaua banake le gaae haai, ve un par daya karenge, aur ve is desh men lautt sakenge kyonki tumhara parameshvar yahova anugrahakaree aur dayalu haai, aur yaadi tum usakee or firoge to vah apana munh tum se n modega.
10 is prakar harakare aepraaim aur manashsho ke deshon men nagar nagar hote huae jabooloon tak gaae parantu unhon ne unakee hansee kee, aur unhen tttthon men udaya.
11 taubhee ashor, manashsho aur jabooloon men se kuchh log deen hokar yarooshalem ko aae.
12 aur yahooda men bhee parameshvar kee aeesee shaaktai hui, ki ve aek man hokar, jo agyaa raja aur hakimon ne yahova ke vachan ke anusar dee thee, use manane ko taaiyar huae.
13 is prakar adhaik log yarooshalem men isaliye ikatthe hua, ki doosare maheene men akhmeeree rottee ka pavrva manen. aur bahut badee sabha ikatthee ho gai.
14 aur unhon ne uttkar, yarooshalem kee veadiyon aur dhoom jalane ke sab sthanon ko uttakar kidraen nale men faenk diya.
15 tab doosare maheene ke chaudahaven din ko unhon ne fasah ke pashu bali kiae tab yajak aur leveey lajjait huae aur apane ko paavitra karake homabaaliyon ko yahova ke bhvan men le aae.
16 aur ve apane niyam ke anuusar, arthata parameshvar ke jan moosa kee vyavastha ke anusar, apane apane sthan par khde hua, aur yajakon ne rakt ko leviyon ke hath se lekar chhdik diya.
17 kyonaki sabha men bahute aeese the jinhon ne apane ko pavitra n kiya tha isaliye sab ashuuddh logon ke fasah ke pashuuon ko bali karane ka adhaikar leaviyon ko diya gaya, ki unako yahova ke liye paavitra karen.
18 bahut se logon ne arthata aepraaim, manashsho, issakar aur jabooloon men se bahuton ne apane ko shuuddh naheen kiya tha, taubhee ve fasah ke pashu ka mans likhee hui vidhai ke viruddh khate the. kyonkee hijaakiyyah ne unake liye yah praarthna kee thee, ki yahova jo bhla haai, vah un sabhon ke pap ddhanp de
19 jo parameshvar kee arthata apane poorvajon ke parameshvar yahova kee khoj men man lagaae huae haai, chahe ve paavitrasthan kee vidhai ke anusar shuuddh n bhee hon.
20 aur yahova ne hijaakiyyah kee yah praarthn sunakar logon ko changa kiya.
21 aur jo israaaelee yarooshalem men upaasthiat the, ve sat din tak akhmeeree rottee ka pavrva bade anand se manate rahe aur praatidin leveey aur yajak unche shabd ke baje yahova ke liye bajakar yahova kee stuti karate rahe.
22 aur jitane leveey yahova ka bhjan buddhimanee ke sath karate the, unako hijaakiyyah ne shaaantai ke vachan kahe. is prakar ve melabaali chaddhakar aur apane purvajon ke parameshvar yahova ke sammukh papangeekar karate rahe aur us niyat pavrva ke saton din tak khate rahe.
23 tab saree sabha ne sammaati kee ki ham aur sat din varva manenge so unhon ne aur sat din anand se pavrva manaya.
24 kyonaki yahooda ke raja hijaakiyyah ne sabha ko aek hajar bachhde aur sat hajar bhed-bakaariyan de dee, aur hakimon ne sabha ko aek hajar bachhde aur das hajar bhed-bakaariyan dee, aur bahut se yajakon ne apane ko paavitra kiya.
25 tab yajakon aur leaviyon samet yahooda kee saree sabha, aur israaael se aae huon kee sabha, aur israaael ke desh se aae hua, aur yahooda men rahanevale paradeshae, in sabhon ne anand kiya.
26 so yarooshalem men bada anand hua, kyonaki daud ke putra israaael ke raja sulaaiman ke dinon se aeesee bat yarooshalem men n hui thee.
27 ant men leveey yajakon ne khde hokar praja ko ashaeervad diya, aur unakee sunee gai, aur unakee praarthna usake papitra dhaam tak arthata svarga tak pahunchee.
