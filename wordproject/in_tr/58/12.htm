Adhyāya 12

1 is karan jab ki gavahon ka aeesa bada badal ham ko ghore huae haai, to ao, har aek rokanevalee vastu, aur ulajhanevale pap ko door karake, vah daud jis meen hamen daudna haai, dhaeeraj se dauden.
2 aur vishvas ke karta aur siddh karanevale yeeshu kee or se takate rahen jis ne us anand ke liye jo usake age dhara tha, lajja kee kuchh chinta n karake, kroos ka dukh saha aur sinhasan par parameshvar ke daahine ja baaitta.
3 isaliye us par dhyan karo, jis ne apane virodha men papiyon ka itana vada-avivad sah liya ki tum nirash hokar hiyav n chhod do.
4 tum ne pap se ladte huae us se aeesee muttbhed naheen kee, ki tumhara lohoo baha ho.
5 aur tum us upadesh ko jo tum ko putraen kee nai diya jata haai, bool gaae ho, ki he mere putra, prabhu kee tadna ko halakee bat n jan, aur jab vah tujhe ghaudke to hiyav n chhod.
6 kyonaki prabhu, jis se praem karata haai, us kee tadna bhee karata haai aur jise putra bana leta haai, us ko kode bhee lagata haai.
7 tum dukh ko tadna samajhkar sah lo: parameshvar tumhen putra janakar tumhare sath bartav karata haai, vah kaun sa putra haai, jis kee tadna pita naheen karata?
8 yaadi vah tadna jis ke bhagee sab hote haai, tumharee naheen hui, to tum putra nahee, par vyaabhaichar kee santan tthare!
9 fir jab ki hamare shaareerik pita bhee hamaree tadna kiya karate the, to kya atmaon ke pita ke aur bhee adhaeen n rahen jis se jeevit rahen.
10 ve to apanee apanee samajh ke anusar thode dinon ke liye tadna karate the, par yah to hamare labh ke liye karata haai, ki ham bhee us kee pavitrata ke bhagee ho jaaen.
11 aur vartaman men har prakar kee tadna anand kee nahee, par shaek hee kee bat dikhai padtee haai, taubhee jo us ko sahate sahate pakke ho gaae haai, peechhe unhen chaain ke sath dharma ka praatifal milata haai.
12 isaliye ddheele hathon aur nirbal ghauttnon ko seedho karo.
13 aur apane panvon ke liye seedho marga banao, ki langada bhttk n jaa, par bhla changa ho jaae..
14 sab se mel milap rakhne, aur us paavitrata ke khojee ho jis ke bina koi prabhu ko kadapi n dekhega.
15 aur dhyan se dekhte raho, aeesa n ho, ki koi parameshvar ke anugrah se vanchit rah jaa, ya koi kadvee jad foottkar kashtt de, aur usake dara bahut se log ashuuddh ho jaaen.
16 aeesa n ho, ki koi jan vyabhaicharee, ya aesav kee nai adharmee ho, jis n aek bar ke bhojan ke badale apane pahilautte hone ka pad bech dala.
17 tum janate to ho, ki bad ko jab us ne ashaeesh panee chahee, to ayogy gina gaya, aur ansoo baha bahakar khojane par bhee man firav ka avasar use n mila..
18 tum to us pahad ke pas jo chooa ja sakata tha aur ag se prajvaalit tha, aur kalee ghatta, aur andhora, aur andhaee ke pasa.
19 aur turahee kee dhvani, aur bolanevale ke aeese shabd ke pas naheen aa, jis ke sunanevalon ne binatee hee, ki ab ham se aur baten n kee jaaen.
20 kyonaki ve us agyaa ko n sah sake, ki yadi koi pashu bhee pahad ko chooa, to patthravah kiya jaae.
21 aur vah darshan aeesa dravana tha, ki moosa ne kaha maain bahut drata aur kanpata hoon.
22 par tum siyyon ke pahad ke pas, aur jeevate parameshvar ke nagar svargeey yarooshalem ke pasa.
23 aur lakhon svargadooton aur un paahilautton kee sadhaaran sabha aur kaleeasiya jin ke nam svarga men likhe huae haain: aur sab ke nyayee parameshvar ke pas, aur siddh kiae huae dhaarmiyon kee atmaon.
24 aur nai vacha ke madhyasth yeeshu, aur chhdikav ke us lohoo ke pas aae ho, jo haabil ke lohoo se uttm baten kahata haai.
25 savadhaan raho, aur us kahanevale se munh n faero, kyonki ve log jab prathvee par ke chitavanee denevale se munh modkar n bach sake, to ham svarga par se chitavanee karanevale se munh modkar kyonkar bach sakenge?
26 us samay to usake shabd ne prathvee ko hila diya par ab us ne yah praatigyaa kee haai, ki ek bar fir maain keval prathvee ko nahee, baran akash ko bhee hila doonga.
27 aur yah vaky 'aek bar firan is bat ko pragatt karata haai, ki jo vastuen hilai jatee haai, ve srajee hui vastuen hone ke karan ttl jaaengee taki jo vastuen hilai naheen jatee, ve attl banee rahen.
28 is karan ham is rajy ko pakar jo hilane ka nahee, us anugrah ko hath se n jane de, jis ke dara ham bhaktai, aur bhy sahit, parameshvar kee aeesee aradhana kar sakate haain jis se vah prasann hota haai.
29 kyonaki hamara parameshvar bhsm karanevalee ag haai..
