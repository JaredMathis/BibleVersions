Adhyāya 4

1 he meree piry too sundar haai, too sundar haai! teree ankhen teree latton ke beech men kabootaron kee see dikhai detee haai. tere bal un bakariyon ke jhund ke saman haain jo gilad pahad ke ddhal par lettee hui hon.
2 tere dant un un kataree hui bhedon ke jhund ke saman haai, jo nahakar upar ain ho, un men har aek ke do do judva bachche hote haain. aur un men se kisee ka sakshaee naheen mara.
3 tere hontt lal rang kee doree ke saman haai, aur tera munh manohar haai, tere kapol teree latton ke neeche anar kee faak se dekh padte haain.
4 tera gala daud ke gummatt ke saman haai, jo asr-shasr ke liye bana ho, aur jis par hajar ddhalen ttngee hui ho, ve sab ddhalen shooraveeron kee haain.
5 teree donon chhaatiyan mrag ke do judve bachchon ke tuly haai, jo sosan foolon ke beech men charate hon.
6 jab tak din ttnda n ho, aur chhaya lambee hote hote mitt n jaa, tab tak maain shaeegharta se gandharas ke pahad aur loban kee pahadee par chala jaunga.
7 he meree piry too sarvang sundaree haai tujh men koi dosh naheen.
8 he meree dulhain, too mere sang labanon se, mere sang labanon se chalee a. too amana kee chottee par se, shaneer aur hermon kee chottee par se, sihon kee gufaaon se, chiton ke pahadon par se drashtti kara.
9 he meree bahin, he meree dulhain, too ne mera man moh liya haai, too ne apanee ankhon kee aek hee chitavan se, aur apane gale ke aek hee heere se mera day moh liya haai.
10 he meree bahin, he meree dulhain, tera praem kya hee manohar haai! tera praem dakhmadha se kya hee uttm haai, aur tere itraen ka sugandha is prakar ke masalon ke sugandha se!
11 he meree dulhain, tere hotton se madha ttpakata haai teree jeebh ke neeche madha or doodha rahata haai teree jeebh ke neeche madha aur doodha rahata haai tere vasren ka sugandha labanon ka sa haai.
12 meree bahin, meree dulhain, kivad lagai hui baree ke saman, kivad band kiya hua sota, or chhap lagaya hua jhrana haai.
13 tere ankur uttm falavalee anar kee baree ke tuly haai, jis men menhadee aur sumbul,
14 jattamasee aur kesar, loban ke sab bhanti ke ped, mushk aur dalacheenee, gandharas, agar, adi sab mukhy mukhy sugandhadravy hote haain.
15 too baariyon ka sota haai, foottte huae jal ka kua, aur labanon se bahatee hui dhaaraaen haain..
16 he uttr vayu jag, aur he dakkhianee vayu chalee a! meree baree par bah, jis se usaka sugandha faaile. mera praemee apanee baree men aye, aur usake uttm uttm fal khaae..
