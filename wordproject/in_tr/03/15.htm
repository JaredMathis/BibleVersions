Adhyāya 15

1 fir yahova ne moosa se kaha,
2 ki israaeliyon se kaho, ki jis jis puroosh ke prameh ho, to vah prameh ke karan se ashuuddh tthare.
3 aur chahe bahata rahe, chahe bahana band bhee ho, taubhee usakee ashuuddhta banee rahegee.
4 jisake prameh ho vah jis jis bichhaune par lette vah ashuuddh tthare, aur jis jis vastu par vah baaitte vah bhee ashuuddh tthare.
5 aur jo koi usake bichhaune ko chooae vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh tthara rahe.
6 aur jisake prameh ho aur vah jis vastu par baaitta ho, us par jo koi baaitte vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh tthara rahe.
7 aur jisake prameh ho us se jo koi choo jaae vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
8 aur jisake prameh ho yaadi vah kisee shuuddh manushy par tooke, to vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
9 aur jisake prameh ho vah savaree kee jis vastu par baaitte vah ashuuddh tthare.
10 aur jo koi kisee vastu ko jo usake neeche rahee ho chooae vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
11 aur jisake prameh ho vah jis kisee ko bina hath dhaeae chooae vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
12 aur jisake prameh ho vah mittee ke jis kisee patra ko chooae vah tod dala jaa, aur katt ke sab prakar ke patra jal se dhaeae jaaen.
13 fir jisake prameh ho vah jab apane rog se changa ho jaa, tab se shuuddh ttharane ke sat din gin le, aur unake beetane par apane vasren ko dhaekar bahate huae jal se snan kare tab vah shuuddh ttharega.
14 aur attven din vah do panduk va kabootaree ke do bachche lekar milapavale tamboo ke dar par yahova ke sammukh jakar unhen yajak ko de.
15 tab yajak un men se aek ko papabali aur doosare ko homabaali ke liye bhentt chaddhaae aur yajak usake liye usake prameh ke karan yahova ke samhane praayashchaitt kare..
16 fir yadi kisee puroosh ka veeyrya skhlit ho jaa, to vah apane sare shareer ko jal se dhaea, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
17 aur jis kisee vasr va chamade par vah veeyrya pade vah jal se dhaeya jaa, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
18 aur jab koi puroosh sree se prasang kare, to ve dono jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahen..
19 fir jab koi sree retumatee rahe, to vah sat din tak ashuuddh ttharee rahe, aur jo koi usako chooae vah sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
20 aur jab tak vah ashuuddh rahe tab tak jis jis vastu par vah lette, aur jis jis vastu par vah baaitte ve sab ashuuddh ttharen.
21 aur jo koi usake bichhaune ko chooae vah apane vasr dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
22 aur jo koi kisee vastu ko chooae jis par vah baaittee ho vah apane vasr dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
23 aur yaadi bichhaune va aur kisee vastu par jis par vah baaittee ho choone ke samay usaka roodhair laga ho, to choonehara sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
24 aur yaadi koi puroosh us se prasang kare, aur usaka roodhair usake lag jaa, to vah puroosh sat din tak ashuuddh rahe, aur jis jis bichhaune par vah lette ve sab ashuuddh ttharen..
25 fir yadi kisee sree ke apane maasik dharma ke niyukt samay se aadhaik din tak roodhair bahata rahe, va us niyukt samay se aadhaik samay tak retumatee rahe, to jab tak vah aeesee dasha men rahe tab tak vah ashuuddh ttharee rahe.
26 usake retumatee rahane ke sab dinon men jis jis bichhaune par vah lette ve sab usake maasik dharma ke bichhaune ke saman ttharen aur jis jis vastu par vah baaitte ve bhee usake retumatee rahe ke dinon kee nai ashuuddh ttharen.
27 aur jo koi un vastuon ko chhuae vah ashuuddh tthare, isaliye vah apane vasren ko dhaekar jal se snan kare, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
28 aur jab vah sree apane retumatee se shuuddh ho jaa, tab se vah sat din gin le, aur un dinon ke beetane par vah shuuddh tthare.
29 fir attven din vah do panduk ya kabootaree ke do bachche lekar milapavale tamboo ke dar par yajak ke pas jaae.
30 tab yajak aek ko papabali aur doosare ko homabaali ke liye chaddhaae aur yajak usake liye usake maasik dharma kee ashuuddhta ke karan yahova ke samhane praayaashchaitt kare..
31 is prakar se tum israaeliyon ko unakee ashuuddhta se nyare rakha karo, kaheen aeesa n ho ki ve yahova ke nivas ko jo unake beech men haai ashuuddh karake apanee ashuuddhta men fanse huae mar jaaen..
32 jisake prameh ho aur jo puroosh veeyrya skhlit hone se ashuuddh ho
33 aur jo sree retumatee ho aur kya puroosh kya sre, jis kisee ke dhaaturog ho, aur jo puroosh ashuuddh sree ke prasang kare, in sabhon ke liye yahee vyavastha haai..
