Adhyāya 11

1 fir yahova ne moosa aur haroon se kaha,
2 israaeliyon se kaho, ki jitane pashu prathvee par haain un sabhon men se tum in jeevadhaariyon ka mans kha sakate ho.
3 pashuuon men se jitane chire va fatte khur ke hote haain aur pagur karate haain unhen kha sakate ho.
4 parantu pagur karanevale va fatte khuravalon men se in pashuuon ko n khana, arthata unt, jo pagur to karata haai parantu chire khur ka naheen hota, isaliye vah tumhare liye ashuuddh tthara haai.
5 aur shaapan, jo pagur to karata haai parantu chire khur ka naheen hota, vah bhee tumhare liye ashuuddh haai.
6 aur khraha, jo pagur to karata haai parantu chire khur ka naheen hota, isaliye vah bhee tumhare liye ashuuddh haai.
7 aur sooar, jo chire arthata fatte khur ka hota to haai parantu pagur naheen karata, isaaliye vah tumhare liye ashuuddh haai.
8 inake mans men se kuchh n khana, aur inakee loth ko choona bhee naheen ye to tumhare liye ashuuddh haai..
9 fir jitane jalajantu haain un men se tum inhen kha sakate ho, arthata samudra va nadiyon ke jalajantuon men se jitanon ke pankh aur chonyette hote haain unhen kha sakate ho.
10 aur jalacharee praaanaiyon men se jitane jeevadhaaree bina pankh aur chonyette ke samudra va naadiyon men rahate haain ve sab tumhare liye gharanait haain.
11 ve tumhare liye gharanait ttharen tum unake mans men se kuchh n khana, aur unakee lothon ko ashuuddh janana.
12 jal men jis kisee jantu ke pankh aur chonyette naheen hote vah tumhare liye ashuuddh haai..
13 fir pakshiayon men se inako ashuuddh janana, ye ashuuddh hone ke karan khaae n jaae, arthata ukab, hadfaod, kurar,
14 shaahee, aur bhanti bhanati kee cheel,
15 aur bhanti bhanati ke sab kag,
16 shuuturmurga, takhmas, jalakukkut, aur bhanti bhanati ke baj,
17 havasil, hadgeel, ullo,
18 rajahas, dhanesha, gidd,
19 lagalag, bhanati bhanati ke bagule, ttttieeharee aur chamageedad..
20 jitane pankhvale char panvon ke bal charate haain ve sab tumhare liye ashuuddh haain.
21 par renganevale aur pankhvale jo char panvon ke bal chalate haai, jinake boomi par koodane faandane ko ttange hotee haain unako to kha sakate ho.
22 ve ye haai, arthata bhanati bhanati kee ttddaie, bhanti bhanati ke fanage, bhanati bhanati ke hargol, aur bhanti bhanati ke hagaba.
23 parantu aur sab renganevale pankhvale jo char panvavale hote haain ve tumhare liye ashuuddh haain..
24 aur inake karan tum ashuuddh ttharoge jis kisee se inakee loth choo jaae vah sanjh tak ashuuddh tthare.
25 aur jo koi inakee loth men ka kuchh bhee uttaae vah apane vasr dhaeae aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
26 fir jitane pashu chire khur ke hote haai. parantu n to bilakul fatte khur aur pagur karanevale haain ve tumhare liye ashuuddh haain jo koi unhen chooae vah ashuuddh haain jo koi unhen chooae vah ashuuddh ttharega.
27 aur char panv ke bal chalanevalon men se jitane panjon ke bal chalate haain ve sab tumhare liye ashuuddh haain jo koi unakee loth chooae vah sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
28 aur jo koi unakee loth uttaae vah apane vasr dhaeae aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe kyonaki ve tumhare liye ashuuddh haain..
29 aur jo prathvee par rengate haain un men se ye renganevale tumhare liye ashuuddh haai, arthata nevala, chooha, aur bhanati bhanati ke goh,
30 aur chhpaikalee, magar, ttkaittkai, sanda, aur giraagittana.
31 sab renganevalon men se ye hee tumhare liye ashuuddh haain jo koi inakee loth chooae vah sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
32 aur in men se kisee kee loth jis kisee vastu par pad jaae vah bhee ashuuddh tthare, chahe vah katt ka koi patra ho, chahe vasr, chahe khal, chahe bora, chahe kisee kam ka kaaisa hee patraaadi kyon n ho vah jal men dala jaa, aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe, tab shuuddh samajha jaae.
33 aur yaadi mittee ka koi patra ho jis men in jantuon men se koi pade, to us patra men jo kuchh ho vah ashuuddh tthare, aur patra ko tum tod dalana.
34 us men jo khane ke yogy bhojan ho, jis men panee ka chhuav hon vah sab ashuuddh tthare fir yadi aeese patra men peene ke liye kuchh ho to vah bhee ashuuddh tthare.
35 aur yaadi inakee loth men ka kuchh tandoor va choolhe par pade to vah bhee ashuuddh tthare, aur tod dala jaae kyonki vah ashuuddh ho jaaega, vah tumhare liye bhee ashuuddh tthare.
36 parantu sota va talab jis men jal ikattha ho vah to shuuddh hee rahe parantu jo koi inakee loth ko chooae vah ashuuddh tthare.
37 aur yaadi inakee loth men ka kuchh kisee prakar ke beej par jo bone ke liye ho pade, to vah beej shuuddh rahe
38 par yaadi beej par jal dala gaya ho aur peechhe loth men ka kuchh us par pad jaa, to vah tumhare liye ashuuddh tthare..
39 fir jin pashuuon ke khane kee agyaa tum ko dee gai haai yadi un men se koi pashu mare, to jo koi usakee loth chooae vah sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
40 aur usakee loth men se jo koi kuchh khaae vah apane vasr dhaeae aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe aur jo koi usakee loth uttaae vah bhee apane vasr dhaeae aur sanjh tak ashuuddh rahe.
41 aur sab prakar ke prathvee par renganevale jantu ghainaune haain ve khaae n jaaen.
42 prathvee par sab renganevalon men se jitane pett va char panvon ke bal chalate haai, va adhaik panvavale hote haai, unhen tum n khana kyonki ve ghainaune haain.
43 tum kisee prakar ke renganevale jantu ke dara apane ap ko ghainauna n karana aur n unake dara apane ko ashuuddh karake apavitra ttharana.
44 kyonaki maain tumhara parameshvar yahova hoon is prakar ke renganevale jantu ke dara jo prathvee par chalata haai apane ap ko ashuuddh n karana.
45 kyonaki maain vah yahova hoon jo tumhen misr desh se isaliye nikal le aya hoon ki tumhara parameshvar ttharoon isaaliye tum paavitra bano, kyonki maain paavitra hoon..
46 pashuuo, pakshiayo, aur sab jalacharee praanaiyo, aur prathvee par sab renganevale praanaiyon ke vishay men yahee vyavastha haai,
47 ki shuuddh ashuuddh aur bhkshay aur abhkshay jeevadhaaariyon men bhed kiya jaae..
