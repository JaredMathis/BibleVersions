Adhyāya 21

1 fir yahova ne moosa se kaha, haroon ke putra jo yajak haai un se kah, ki tumhare logon men se koi bhee mare, to usake karan tum men se koi apane ko ashuuddh n kare
2 apane nikatt kuttumbaiyo, arthata apanee mata, va pita, va bette, va bettee, va bhai ke liye,
3 va apanee kunvaree bahin jisaka vivah n hua ho jinaka sameepee sambandha haai unake liye vah apane ko ashuuddh kar sakata haai.
4 par yajak hone ke nate se vah apane logon men pradhaan haai, isaaliye vah apane ko aeesa ashuuddh n kare ki apaavitra ho jaae.
5 ve n to apane sir mundaae, aur n apane gal ke balon ko mundaae, aur n apane shareer cheeren.
6 ve apane parameshvar ke liye paavitra bane rahe, aur apane parameshvar ka nam apaavitra n karen kyonki ve yahova ke havy ko jo unake parameshvar ka bhojan haai chaddhaya karate haain is karan ve pavitra bane rahen.
7 ve veshya va bhrashtta ko byah n len aur n tyagee hui ko byah len kyonki yajak apane parameshvar ke liye paavitra hota haai.
8 isaliye too yajak ko pavitra janana, kyonaki vah tumhare parameshvar ka bhojan chaddhaya karata haai isaaliye vah teree drashtti men pavitra tthare kyonaki maain yahova, jo tum ko paavitra karata hoo, pavitra hoon.
9 aur yaadi yajak kee bettee veshya banakar apane ap ko apaavitra kare, to vah apane pita ko apavitra ttharatee haai vah ag men jalai jaae..
10 aur jo apane bhaiyon men mahayajak ho, jisake sir par abhaishok ka tel dala gaya ho, aur jisaka pavitra vasren ko paahinane ke liye sanskar hua ho, vah apane sir ke bal bikhrane n de, aur apane vasr faade
11 aur n vah kisee loth ke pas jaa, aur n apane pita va mata ke karan apane ko ashuuddh kare
12 aur vah paavitrasthan se bahar bhee n nikale, aur n apane parameshvar ke paavitrasthan ko apavitra ttharaae kyonaki vah apane parameshvar ke abhaishok ka telaroopee mukutt dhaaran kiae huae haai maain yahova hoon.
13 aur vah kunvaree hee sree ko byahe.
14 jo vidhava, va tyagee hui, va bhrasht, va veshya ho, aeesee kisee ko vah n byahe, vah apane hee logon ke beech men kee kisee kunvaree kanya ko byahe
15 aur vah apane veeyrya ko apane logon men apaavitra n kare kyonaki maain usaka paavitra karanevala yahova hoon.
16 fir yahova ne moosa se kaha,
17 haroon se kah, ki tere vansh kee peeddhee peeddhee men jis kisee ke koi bhee dosh hon vah apane parameshvar ka bhojan chaddhane ke liye sameep n aae.
18 koi kyon n ho jis men dosh ho vah sameep n aa, chahe vah andha ho, chahe langada, chahe nakachapatta ho, chahe usake kuchh adhaik ang ho,
19 va usaka panv, va hath tootta ho,
20 va vah kubada, va bauna ho, va usakee ankh men dosh ho, va us manushy ke chain va khjulee ho, va usake and pichake hon
21 haroon yajak ke vansh men se jis kisee men koi bhee dosh ho vah yahova ke havy chaddhane ke liye sameep n aae vah jo doshayukt haai kabhee bhee apane parameshvar ka bhojan chaddhane ke liye sameep n aae.
22 vah apane parameshvar ke paavitra aur paramapavitra donon prakar ke bhojan ko khaa,
23 parantu usake dosh ke karan vah n to beechavale parde ke bheetar aae aur n vedee ke sameep, jis se aeesa n ho ki vah mere paavitrasthanon ko apaavitra kare kyonki maain unaka paavitra karanevala yahova hoon.
24 isaliye moosa ne haroon aur usake putraen ko tatha kul israaeliyon ko yah baten kah sunain..
