Adhyāya 51

1 he dharma par chalanevalo, he yahova ke ddoonddhnevalo, kan lagakar meree suno jis chattan men se tum khode gaae aur jis khani men se tum nikale gaa, us par dhyan karo.
2 apane moolapuroosh ibraaheem aur apanee mata sara par dhyan karo jab vah akela tha, tab hee se maain ne usako bulaya aur ashaeesh dee aur baddha diya.
3 yahova ne siyyon ko shaaantai dee haai, us ne usake sab khndharon ko shaantai dee haai vah usake jangal ko adan ke saman aur usak nirjal desh ko yahova kee battkai ke saman banaaega us men harsha aur anand aur dhanyavad aur bhjan gane ka shabd sunai padega..
4 he meree praja ke logo, meree or dhyan dharo he mere logo, kan lagakar meree suno kyonaki meree or se vyavastha dee jaaegee, aur maain apana niyam desh desh ke logon kee jyoti hone ke liye sthiar karoonga.
5 mera chhuttkara nikatt haai mera uddhar pragatt hua haai maain apane bhujabal se desh desh ke logon ka nyay karoonga. deep meree batt jahenge aur mere bhujabal par asha rakhenge.
6 akash kee or apanee ankhen uttao, aur prathvee ko niharo kyonki akash dhaunae hee nai lop ho jaaega, prathvee kapade ke saman puranee ho jaaegee, aur usake rahanevale yon hee jate rahenge parantu jo uddhar maain karoonga vah sarvada ttharega, aur mere dharma ka ant n hoga..
7 he dharma ke jananevalo, jinake man men meree vyavastha haai, tum kan lagakar meree suno manushyon kee namadharai se mat dro, aur unake ninda karane se vismait n ho.
8 kyonaki dhaun unhen kapade kee nain aur keeda unhen un kee nain khaaega parantu mera dharma anantakal tak, aur mera uddhar peeddhee se peeddhee tak bana rahega.
9 he yahova kee bhuja, jag ! jag aur bal dhaaran kara jaaise praacheenakal men aur beete huae peeddhiyon me, vaaise hee ab bhee jaga. kya too vahee naheen haai jis ne rahab ko ttukade ttukade kiya aur magaramachchh ko chheda?
10 kya too vahee naheen jis ne samudra ko arthata gahire sagar ke jal ko sukha dala aur usakee gaharai men apane chhudaae haon ke par jane ke liye marga nikala tha?
11 so yahova ke chhudaae huae log lauttkar jayajayakar karate huae siyyon men aaenge, aur unake siron par anant anand goonjata rahega ve harsha aur anand praapt karenge, aur shaek aur sisaakiyon ka ant ho jaaega..
12 maai, maain hee tera shaaantaidata hoon too kaun haai jo maranevale manushy se, aur ghaas ke saman murjhanevale adamee se drata haai,
13 aur akash ke tananevale aur prathvee kee nev dalanevale apane karta yahova ko bool gaya haai, aur jab draehee nash karane ko taaiyar hota haai tab usakee jalajalahatt se din bhr lagatar thrathrata haai? parantu draehee kee jalajalahatt kahan rahee?
14 bandhaua shaeegha hee svatantr kiya jaaega vah gadhe men n marega aur n use rottee kee kamee hogee.
15 jo samudra ko uthla-puthl karata jis se usakee laharon me garajan hotee haai, vah maain hee tera parameshvar yahova hoon mera nam senaon ka yahova haai. aur maain ne tere munh men apane vachan dale,
16 aur tujhe apane hath kee ad men chhpai rakha haai ki maain akash ko tanoon aur prathvee kee nev daloo, aur siyyon se kahoo, tum meree praja ho..
17 he yarooshalem jag ! jag utt ! khdee ho ja, too ne yahova ke hath se usakee jalajalahatt ke kattore men se piya haai, too ne kattore ka ladkhda denevala mad poora poora hee pee liya haai.
18 jitane ladkon ne us se janm liya un men se koi n raha jo usakee aguvai karake le chale aur jitane ladke us ne pale-pose un men se koi n raha jo usake hath ko tham le.
19 ye do vipaatiayan tujh par a padee haain kaun tere sang vilap karega? ujad aur vinash aur mahangee aur talavar a padee haai kaun tujhe shaantai dega?
20 tere ladke moochchhirt hokar har aek sadk ke sire par, mahajal men fanse huae haarin kee nai pade haain yahova kee jalajalahatt aur tere parameshvar kee dhamakee ke karan ve achet pade haain..
21 is karan he dukhaiyaree sun, too matavalee to haai, parantu dakhmadha peekar naheen
22 tera prabhu yahova jo apanee praja ka mukama ladnevala tera parameshvar haai, vah yon kahata haai, sun maain ladkhda denevale mad ke kattore ko arthata apanee jalajalahatt ke kattore ko tere hath se le leta hoon tujhe us men se fir kabhee peena n padega.
23 aur maain use tere un du:kh denevalon ke hath men doonga, jinhon ne tujh se kaha, lett ja, ki ham tujh par panv dharakar age chalen aur too ne aundho munh girakar apanee peett ko boomi aur age chalanevalon ke liye sadk bana diya..
