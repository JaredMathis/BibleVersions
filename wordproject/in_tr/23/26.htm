Adhyāya 26

1 us samay yahooda desh men yah geet gaya jaega, hamara ek draddh nagar haai uddhar ka kam dene ke liye vah usakee shaharapanah aur gaddh ko niyukt karata haai.
2 faattkon ko kholo ki sachchai ka palan karanevalee aek dharmee jaati pravesh kare.
3 jisaka man tujh men dhaeeraj dhare huae haai, usakee too poorn shaaantai ke sath raksha karata haai, kyonki vah tujh par bhrosa rakhta haai.
4 yahova par sada bhrosa rak, kyonaki prabhu yahova sanatan chattan haai.
5 vah unche padavale ko jhuka deta, jo nagar unche par basa haai usako vah neeche kar deta. vah usako boomi par girakar mittee men mila deta haai.
6 vah panvon se, varan daridraen ke paairon se raunda jaaega..
7 dharmee ka marga sachchai haai too jo svayan sachchai haai, too dharmee kee aguvai karata haai.
8 he yahova, tere nyay ke marga men ham log teree batt johate aae haain tere nam ke smaran kee hamare praanon men lalasa banee rahatee haai.
9 rat ke samay maain jee se taree lalasa karata hoo, mera sampoorn man se yatn ke sath tujhe ddoonddhta haai. kyonki jab tere nyay ke kam prathvee par pragatt hote haai, tab jagat ke rahanevale dharma kee seekhte haain.
10 dushtt par chahe daya bhee kee jaae taubhee vah dharma ko n seekhega dharmarajy men bhee vah kuattlaita karega, aur yahova ko mahatmy use soojh n padega..
11 he yahova, tera hath baddha hua haai, par ve naheen dekhte. parantu ve janenge ki tujhe praja ke liye kaaisee jalan haai, aur lajaaenge.
12 tere baairee ag se bhsm honge. he yahova, too hamare liye shaantai ttharaaega, ham ne jo kuchh kiya haai use too hee ne hamare liye kiya haai.
13 he hamare parameshvar yahova, tere sivay aur svamee bhee ham par prabhuta karate the, parantu teree krapa se ham keval tere hee nam ka gunanuvad karenge.
14 ve mar gaae haai, fir kabhee jeeavit naheen honge unako mare bahut din hua, ve fir naheen uttne ke too ne unaka vichar karake unako aeesa nash kiya ki ve fir smaran men n aaenge.
15 parantu too ne jati ko baddhaya he yahova, too ne jaati ko baddhaya haai too ne apanee mahima dikhai haai aur us desh ke sab sivanon ko too ne baddhaya haai..
16 he yahova, du:kh men ve tujhe smaran karate the, jab too unhen tadna deta tha tab ve dabe svar se apane man kee bat tujh par pragatt karate the.
17 jaaise garbhvatee sree janane ke samay aeentttee aur peedon ke karan chilla utttee haai, ham log bhee, he yahova, tere samhane vaaise hee ho gaae haain.
18 ham bhee garbhvatee hua, ham bhee aeentte, ham ne mano vayu hee ko janm diya. ham ne desh ke liye koi uddhar ka kam naheen kiya, aur n jagat ke rahanevale utpann huae.
19 tere mare huae log jeevit honge, murde utt khde honge. he mittee men basanevalo, jagakar jayajayakar karo! kyonki teree os jyoti se utpann hotee haai, aur prathvee murdo ko lautta degee..
20 he mere logo, ao, apanee apanee kottree men pravesh karake kivadon ko band karo thodee der tak jab tak krodha shaant n ho tab tak apane ko chhpai rakho.
21 kyonaki dekho, yahova prathvee nivaasiyon ko adharma ka dand dene ke liye apane sthan se chala ata haai, aur prathvee apana koon pragatt karegee aur ghaat kiae huon ko aur aadhaik n chhpai rakhegee..
